IT Security Consulting

Defensive and offensive computer consulting. Specializing in computer defense audits and operational security for sensitive data and systems.

Today's environment leaves no room for lax security. Not only are there heavy fines for the loss of sensitive data, it can also mean loss of trade secrets or the lives of journalistic sources. Unfortunately, configuring and maintaining a secure system is an extremely challenging task. Let me help you find the right balance between security and usability for your environment.

An IT Professional for IT Professionals.

Working with disparate computer systems is hard, no one IT person is an expert in all the latest technologies. The best we can hope to get is a broad understanding of the systems in our environment and sometimes a deep understanding of some of them.

My deep experience is in web programming on Windows with C#, SQL Server database systems, and informatoin security. In my free time, I study security defense and theory as well as the economic and political ramifications of a networked world.

Over the past fifteen years, I've worked on UNIX, Linux, Windows, and Mac operating systems. These systems have all been deployed in production environments. My philosophy is to pick the best system for the customers requirements..
